Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is Product Sourcing?
Product sourcing is the process of finding trustworthy manufacturers or suppliers who can produce or deliver quality products that fit your business needs.
What does a sourcing company do?
A sourcing company identifies reliable manufacturers, develops samples, negotiates prices and terms, supervises production quality, and manages global shipping.
How do you identify qualified suppliers?
We use our extensive database of manufacturers to match your exact requirements. Each potential supplier is evaluated for capability, quality, and reliability before we negotiate optimal pricing and terms.
What quality checks are involved?
Samples are approved before production. Routine inspections are done during manufacturing. Final inspection is done before shipping along with spot checks.
Do I need to order a full container?
No. Depending on your product and order volume, you can choose part-load, mixed-load, or full-container shipping options.
Is there a minimum order quantity?
Yes, most products have an MOQ, but the quantity varies by item.
Do shipments always arrive on schedule?
We aim for timely deliveries, but unexpected factors such as vessel delays, weather, or customs procedures may occasionally impact shipping timelines.
What shipping terms do you follow?
We work with your preferred shipping terms, including Free On Board (FOB), Cost and Freight (CFR), and Cost, Insurance & Freight (CIF).
